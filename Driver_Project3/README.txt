Driver Project 3

Holding button 1 will blink the LED for 1 second on then 1 second off and display that button 1 is being pressed
Holding button 2 will blink the LED for 2 seconds on then 2 seconds off and display that button 2 is being pressed
Holding button 3 will blink the LED for 3 seconds on then 3 seconds off and display that button 3 is being pressed
Holding 2 or more buttons will keep the LED on and will display a message showing which two buttons are being pressed
Holding all 3 buttons will keep the LED on and will display a message saying that all three buttons are being pressed
No buttons being pressed will keep the LED off and show a message saying that no buttons are being pressed
